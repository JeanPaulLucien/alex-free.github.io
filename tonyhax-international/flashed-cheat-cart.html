How To Flash Your GameShark/Action Reply/Cheat Cart

Inside each Tonyhax International release in the rom directory are the following files:

  • tonyhax-rom-flasher.bin
  • tonyhax-rom-flasher.cue

The BIN+CUE files can be burned to a CD-R. The BIN+CUE files contain the NXFlash PS1 executable with the tonyhax-*-international.rom file. This means when you boot the burned flasher CD-R on your PS1 console, you can connect your GameShark/cheat device and press the R2 button (RE-DETECT EEPROM) to allow NXFLASH to detect the newly connected cheat cart. Next press the start button (FLASH EEPROM FROM CD), and then press X to flash your GameShark/Cheat device. Now you can simply reset or power off then power on your PS1 console and it will boot Tonyhax International from the GameShark/cheat cart immediately.
